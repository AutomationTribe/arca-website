Payment terminal service
provider (PTSP)

ARCA PTSP comprises of different aspects which are: POS
hardware, POS SDK, and Arca POS app. Arca configures,
deploys and maintains POS terminals for Financial Institutions.
Arca has also been able to build a terminal application to allow
merchant accept payment via cash, card, etc. on an android
powered POS terminal and Linux terminals.

SERVICES IN PTSP:

Android and Linux version

a terminal app to allow a merchant accept payment via cash, card, USSD
and QR Codes to support normal merchants’ operation

Software Development Kit (SDK)

with our SDK device rendering, partnered with the POS terminals per
product, we have simplified the payment process for third parties who may
require a plug-in into these services to perform card transactions.

SPECIALIZED COLLECTION SERVICES OPERATIONS

a customized app to suit the needs of certain merchants eg. Hospitals,
churches, schools etc

CASH OUT AND CASH IN OPERATIONS

addresses Super Agents’, need to route Cash-in and Cash-out transactions
through Arca switching infrastructure

WHO IS IT FOR

Arca POS is available for use for Financial Institutions e.g. Commercial banks,
Microfinance banks, churches, hospitals, schools and fintechs.
