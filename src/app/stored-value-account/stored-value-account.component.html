Stored Value Account

SVA is an enterprise-wide payment system to help Corporates
carry out third party disbursements of funds from their bank
account to beneficiary(s) with different bank accounts in any of
the 22 banks in Nigeria, in the most secure and convenient way possible.

WHO IS IT FOR

SVA is available for use for Non-Financial Institutions e.g. Public
Institutions (Government), NGOs, Private Institutions.

HOW DOES IT HELP BUSINESSES OR ENTERPRISES

It enables NFIs carry out single disbursements such as salaries,
vendors and suppliers payment.
