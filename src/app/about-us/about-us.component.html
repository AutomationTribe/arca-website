Who We Are

We are building next-generation products and services for Africa’s
thriving payments ecosystem. Arca is on a mission to enable
financial institutions, banks, and SMEs to collect and process
payments from their customers.

We are building tools to empower business owners and make it
easier for them to get paid online and offline. Our suite of products
and solutions are designed to function seamlessly in our current
fragmented payments ecosystem. We are working to connect
individuals, financial institutions, banks, and businesses to Africa’s
digital economy.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to become Africa's premier payment services
platform, fostering financial inclusion, continuous
improvement and innovation, actively shaping the future
of Financial Services throughout the continent.

OUR MISSION

Our purpose is to create a feature rich, financial solutions
& services ecosystem, delivered through a open and
flexible digital platforms
